The WTO will host a public Open Day on July 5, 2026, celebrating the 100th anniversary of Centre William Rappard. The event features guided building tours, cultural exhibits, children's activities, and an exhibition co-organized with the International Labour Organization. An opening ceremony in the Council Room will include Director-General Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala and Swiss officials.

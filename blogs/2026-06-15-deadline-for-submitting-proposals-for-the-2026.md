The submission deadline for WTO Public Forum session proposals has been extended to June 17, 2026. The Forum, titled "Powering the Future" and scheduled for September 15-17, welcomes proposals from civil society, academia, business, governments, and intergovernmental organizations. This year's theme focuses on trade in services, with participants able to submit through online application forms.

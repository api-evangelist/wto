The 2026 Global Review, scheduled for October 29-30 in Geneva, will examine how strengthened partnerships can help developing and least-developed countries leverage trade opportunities for economic growth and job creation. The event will gather trade practitioners, ministers, and international organization leaders to discuss global value chain diversification, trade facilitation, services, digital commerce, and net-zero transitions.
